Air Quality & Ductwork

Breathe Easier, Room to Room

Uneven temperatures between rooms are almost always a duct problem, not an equipment problem. We test, seal, and resize ductwork — then add filtration or humidity control where it actually helps.

Duct Sealing

Stop conditioned air leaking into your attic or crawlspace before it reaches a single vent.

Filtration & UV

HEPA-grade filtration and UV-C lights for allergy-sensitive households.

Humidity Control

Whole-home humidifiers and dehumidifiers paired to your system, not a standalone box.

Zoning Systems

About Apex Comfort

Family-Owned. Locally Trusted. Since 2004.

Apex Comfort started as a two-truck operation fixing window units and furnaces around Riverton. Two decades later, we're still locally owned — just with a much bigger toolbox.

Our Story

Founder Ray Delgado started Apex Comfort in 2004 after a decade working for larger regional contractors. He set one rule that still governs every job: explain the problem in plain language, give one honest price, and never sell a part that isn't needed.

Today the team has grown to 14 technicians and three dispatchers, but the rule hasn't changed.
